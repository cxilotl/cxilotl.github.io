Ryoshinkan Dojo

Ryoshinkan Dojo dedicates itself to the traditional Japanese martial arts of Iaido (the quick draw of the sword) and Jodo (the way of the stick).

Training sessions are from 19:00 to 21:00 on Thursdays at Byfleet Village Hall.
